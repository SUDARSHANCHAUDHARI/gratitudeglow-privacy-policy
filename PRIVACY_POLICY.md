# GratitudeGlow Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026
_Last updated: March 21, 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

GratitudeGlow ("we," "our," or "us") is a guided gratitude journaling app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **User Profile**: Name and email address via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or email/password).
- **Authentication**: User ID from Firebase Authentication.

### Journal Data (Synced via Firebase)
- Gratitude journal entries you write, including text and timestamps.
- Journal data is stored in **Firebase Firestore** to sync across your sessions and devices.

## How We Use Your Information

- Authenticate your account securely.
- Sync your journal entries across sessions via Firestore.
- Display your journal history within the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

## Storage and Retention

- Journal entries are stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud infrastructure).
- Data is retained while your account is active.
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time (see Data Deletion section).

## Data Security

- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
- Data is encrypted at rest in Firebase Firestore.
- Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account.

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Authentication** (Google): Secure sign-in.
- **Firebase Firestore** (Google): Cloud storage for journal entries.
- See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) and [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for their data practices.

## Permissions Used

- **INTERNET**: Required to sync journal entries with Firebase.
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: Used to check connectivity before syncing.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** (Android 13+): Required to send daily gratitude reminders.

## Your Rights and Controls

- Edit or delete individual journal entries at any time within the app.
- Sign out of the app at any time.
- Request full account and data deletion (see Data Deletion section).

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data.
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

## Children's Privacy

GratitudeGlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open GratitudeGlow → Settings → Delete Account, or
2. Email sunny.sudarshan@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email.

All journal entries and account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
